What is Ludicrous Swole?

Ludicrous Swole is a very silly game made by two very silly people. In this game, you would have competed with a friend or rival to flex your splendid biceps faster than they did.

Where can I get it?

We've released it on its GitHub page, under the "Releases" section. Unfortunately, we've discontinued the game, but it works as a singleplayer game right now, so you can still try it! If you want to have a demo of it before playing the real deal, we've made handy text-based version of the game, too!
